MORE ABOUT LAIN

ELAINA HART

Lain's pixel art journey began with a love for retro games and their blocky, expressive designs. Self-taught, she honed her skills in creating detailed art with limited palettes, eventually incorporating animation to bring her work to life. What started as a hobby evolved into a passion, leading her to take on personal projects and commissions, always pushing her creative boundaries

// 2 WHAT INSPIRES HER

Lain finds inspiration in the charm of classic 8-bit and 16-bit video games, where simple pixel designs convey rich stories and emotions. This nostalgic influence fuels her passion for creating modern pixel art with a retro touch. Beyond games, Lain draws from the world around her—nature, urban landscapes, and everyday details—all of which shape her vibrant color choices and intricate designs. This blend of retro inspiration and real-world influences allows her to craft unique, story-driven pixel art that feels both timeless and contemporary

Lain primarily uses Aseprite to craft her pixel art, leveraging its intuitive tools for layering and frame-by-frame animation to create detailed, vibrant pieces. For more complex projects, she turns to Photoshop to refine her work and enhance details. She also uses Lospec to manage and optimize her color palettes, ensuring each piece maintains a cohesive and striking visual style. This blend of tools allows Lain to balance creativity with precision, bringing her pixel art visions to life
